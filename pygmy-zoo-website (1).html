About Pygmy Zoo

Home to the world's most adorable miniature animals

Experience Something Unique

Welcome to Pygmy Zoo, Sharjah's most enchanting destination for family fun! We are dedicated to showcasing the incredible world of miniature animals, creating memorable experiences for visitors of all ages.

Our zoo features a carefully curated collection of rare and adorable pygmy animals from around the world. Each animal is cared for with the utmost dedication, and our expert team ensures they thrive in a safe, natural environment.

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning favorite, Pygmy Zoo offers an unforgettable adventure filled with wonder, education, and joy!
